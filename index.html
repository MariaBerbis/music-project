R&B music most of all

R&B music: A very popular and wide-ranging style of music that emerged from traditional rythm and blues in the 1940s. R&B can be characterized by its use of blues chords played over a strong and consistent backbeat and by its emphasis on composition rather than the improvisation common in traditional blues. Hip hop, rap, soul, and disco are all categories of R&B. This music style also formed the foundation of rock and roll.
